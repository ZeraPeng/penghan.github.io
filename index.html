Zero-Shot Skeleton-based Action Recognition

Skeleton Sept 2024 - March 2025

Developed a novel framework using Variational Autoencoders (VAEs) to disentangle skeleton features, enhancing semantic-visual alignment.

Transformed raw skeleton data into semantically meaningful body-part features with multiple partition strategies. Leveraged LLMs to generate detailed part descriptions from action labels.

Designed a computationally efficient MLP ensemble architecture that achieved 4.2% higher accuracy than state-of-the-art methods under challenging zero-shot learning splits on NTU60 and NTU120 Datasets.

Multi-modal Distillation of Egocentric Video Understanding

Video Jan 2024 - May 2024

Applied multimodal KD on FPHA and H2O datasets by matching logits. Trained the Swin-T teacher model by RGB and optical flow, while Swin-T, ResNet18, and ResNet50 served as student models.

Optimized the baseline KD by Transformed Teacher Matching (TTM), which converted student-side balancing parameter adjustments into equivalent teacher-side adjustments.

Aesthetic Evaluation of Works of Art Based on Image Features

Image Aug 2021 - Feb 2023

Built the extended dataset. Selected 4,000 borderless pictures of suitable size and definition from the landscape paintings. 110,163 annotations were collected from campus questionnaires.

Fused deep CNN features with reference-free statistical features generated from Blind/Referenceless Image Spatial Quality Evaluator (BRISQUE).

Compared VGG19, ResNet152, and CLIP, highlighting the BRISQUE-optimized CLIP model. Segmentation correctness reached 93%, with overall correctness at 81%.
